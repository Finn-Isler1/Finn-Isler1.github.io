Home

When we go about our day-to-day lives, we are constantly seeing and experiencing new things. Many of these things will live on in our memory for many years, and sometimes, for our whole lives. Photography helps to keep track of the world around us in a more tangible way. It is used capture and remember the things we see. It is also used to share the way we view the world with other people.

About

My name is Finn Isler and I have been practing hobby photography for about a year or so. I mainly enjoy street photography, landscape photography, and architectural photography. I have more experience with digital photography but I have shot some film.

I grew to enjoy this hobby because I feel that it makes any activity something to look forward, regardless of whether or not I enjoy it. For example, I recently went on a trip to the beach with my dad, brother, and two friends. They are very passionate about fishing but I am not. Regardless, I still got excited to go because I could bring my camera with me and take photos while they fished. Everyone was happy.

Best Times

There is common sentiment that photography is most effective during golden hour. This refers to the hour after sunrise and the hour before sunset. This is because the light casts a sort of golden/orange tint over everything and makes everything look flattering. Shooting during golden hour is definetly a good idea for easy natural light, especially for portraits and landscapes.

Shooting excluseively during golden hour however, can be limitting for many reasons. It does not allow you to experiment with other lighting conditions. This in turn can lead to all of your photographs looking the same way. For documentary style photography, it is both unrealisitc and untrue to the craft. For example, if I am documenting daily life in a particular part of downtown and I only shoot an hour before the sun sets, I am missing a great deal of what actually happens at that location.

How

Nowadays, photography is extremely accessible because of the fact that most people have a camera with them at all times in their pocket. Phone cameras have come a long way and are more than capable of producing high quality images.

While the phone cameras are very capable, there is a always going to be a tactile disconnect when using a phone camera. Why

Expressing the way we view the world in some way or another is very important to the human experience. Photography is a great way to do this because the barrier to entry is virtually non-existent. Anyone is capable of taking a photo. This accessibly is a great thing becaus it means people of all ages and from all backgrounds are able to participate.

Being able to take an effective photo is just a generally useful skill to have as well. It is so common to be asked to take photos of people and of different things, so why not deliver a photo that is superb? For instance, I was a part of the Midas Club here at Charlotte last semester and they put on a fashion show event. I was able to take some of the photos of the event to use as promotion on their social medias.

What to Bring

Packing for a trip abroad can be difficult. While you may be tempted to bring as much stuff as possible because of how long the trip is, you really will be much off only bringing a single bag. One bag can be limiting though so it is important to consider what items you bring.

Essential Items:

  • Sturdy shoes
  • Collapseable seat (see below)
  • Nice clothing
  • Portrable charger
  • Adaptable plug
  • Lanyard for ID and important cards
